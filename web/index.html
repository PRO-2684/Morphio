Morphio

Morphs the font, so one word renders as another. Made with Rust & WebAssembly, designed for speed.

Quick Start

  1. Upload a font.
  2. Add one or more rules.
  3. Click Morph, test the preview, then download.

Recipes

Import or export TOML recipes to reuse rules and advanced settings across fonts and sessions.
